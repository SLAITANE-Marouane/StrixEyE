01  /  About

Drone engineers. Aerial operators.

STRIXEYE engineers, integrates and operates intelligent aerial platforms across three verticals — aerial advertising, fixed-wing UAVs, and aerial cinematography. Years of aviation R&D, Moroccan regulatory relationships, and a full operations stack on the ground.

Not an ad agency. Not a pure drone shop. Both.

We are aviation engineers who treat the sky as infrastructure. We integrate world-class hardware, build the AI and ground-control layer that makes it intelligent, and operate the fleet ourselves — across mega-events, industrial missions, and high-visibility brand activations.

Our pedigree spans three phases: SKYEGG (foundational drone R&D), FLYINCAM (operational RC-helicopter cinematography across Morocco), and STRIXEYE (flying LED billboards and fixed-wing UAV operations). One team. One stack. One sky.

03  /  Platforms

Flying billboards. Long-endurance UAVs.

Two integrated aerial platforms operated end-to-end by STRIXEYE — built around regulatory expertise, AI, and on-the-ground ops in Morocco.

Flagship · Aerial LED Advertising

The flying billboard.

A 10-metre lighter-than-air platform with an integrated 7.4 m² LED display — visible day and night across stadiums, festivals and urban activations. Hours of station-keeping. GPS-targeted, broadcast-ready, impossible to ignore.

  • 10 m envelope · 34 m³ helium · 92 kg packed
  • 2 m × 3.7 m P15 LED · 1,600 cd/m² · IP54
  • 1–2 hours station-keeping · 10 km control link
  • Mobile + USB remote control · live impression analytics
Operating · MENA exclusive
Long-endurance fixed-wing UAV

The Mosquito — patrol & mapping.

A foldable delta-wing platform for long-range patrol, infrastructure inspection and aerial survey. Catapult or runway launch, GPS guidance, dual-light EO + thermal pod, 4K imaging — operated by STRIXEYE in Morocco.

  • 2 m wingspan · 35 kg MTOW · 10 kg payload
  • 5–8 h endurance · up to 1,000 km range
  • 150 km/h max · 4,000 m ceiling · 12 m/s wind
  • Catapult or runway launch · foldable for field deployment
